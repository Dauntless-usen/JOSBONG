Deadlines and rigor don't have to be in tension. We help students and
researchers move faster on the parts of academic work that are
time-consuming rather than the parts that require original thought — data
analysis, structuring, and editing.

## Who this is for

Students and researchers working against a deadline who need a second set
of hands on data analysis, structure, or editing without compromising
academic integrity.

## How we work

We work within your institution's academic integrity guidelines: we help
you analyze, structure, and sharpen your own work — we don't write it for
you to submit as-is. Every engagement starts with a clear scope of what
kind of help is appropriate for your assignment.
