We build AI features that hold up outside a demo — grounded in your actual
data, evaluated against real success metrics, and handed off with the

## Who this is for

Teams that know AI could help somewhere in their product or operations but
need a partner to take it from idea to something shipped.

## How we work

We start by defining what "working" means for your use case, then prototype
quickly against your real data before investing in production
infrastructure. Nothing ships until it's been evaluated against the metrics
we agreed on up front.
