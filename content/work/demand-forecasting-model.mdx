## Challenge

A regional retail chain was over- and under-stocking across 40+ locations
because their forecasting relied on a simple moving average that didn't
account for seasonality or local events.

## Approach

We built a demand forecasting model trained on three years of historical
sales data, weather, and local event calendars, and wired it into their
existing inventory pipeline via a scheduled Airflow job.

## Outcome

Forecast error dropped 32% within the first full quarter, directly
reducing both stockouts and excess inventory carrying costs.
