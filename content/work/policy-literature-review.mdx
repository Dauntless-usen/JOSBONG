## Challenge

A policy institute needed an up-to-date literature review on a fast-moving
regulatory topic to brief their board, but their small research team
didn't have the bandwidth.

## Approach

We ran a systematic review across academic and policy sources, screened
for relevance and quality, and synthesized the findings into a structured
brief organized around the board's key open questions.

## Outcome

The brief synthesized over 120 sources into a document the board could
read in one sitting, fully cited for follow-up.
