## Challenge

A research lab was manually cleaning survey responses in spreadsheets,
which took several days per wave and introduced inconsistent handling of
missing data.

## Approach

We built a repeatable data pipeline that ingests raw survey exports,
applies consistent cleaning and validation rules, and loads the result
into a Postgres database ready for analysis.

## Outcome

What used to take several days of manual spreadsheet work now runs in
under two hours, with a consistent, auditable set of cleaning rules
applied every time.
