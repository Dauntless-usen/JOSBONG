## Challenge

A graduate student had collected survey data for their thesis but was
running out of time to run and interpret the statistical analysis before
their submission deadline.

## Approach

We reviewed the research questions and hypotheses, ran the appropriate
statistical tests in R, and produced a plain-language writeup the student
could use directly in their results chapter.

## Outcome

The student submitted their thesis two weeks ahead of deadline with a
complete, defensible results section.
