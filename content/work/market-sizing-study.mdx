## Challenge

A SaaS startup was deciding whether to expand into a new vertical but had
no reliable estimate of the addressable market or the competitive
landscape.

## Approach

We combined secondary research, public filings, and a set of structured
interviews with industry practitioners to build a bottom-up market size
estimate, cross-checked against top-down industry reports.

## Outcome

The resulting report gave the leadership team enough confidence in the
market size to greenlight a 7-figure expansion into the new vertical.
