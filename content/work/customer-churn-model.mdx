## Challenge

A subscription company only found out about churn after customers had
already cancelled, leaving no window for retention outreach.

## Approach

We trained a churn prediction model on product usage and billing data,
and built a dashboard that surfaces at-risk accounts to the customer
success team every week.

## Outcome

The model now flags 68% of eventual churners a full 30 days before
cancellation, giving the success team a real window to intervene.
