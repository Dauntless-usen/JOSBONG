## Challenge

Regional sales leads relied on a manually assembled spreadsheet report
that was often several days out of date by the time it reached them.

## Approach

We modeled the underlying sales data in SQL and built a live dashboard
that regional leads could filter and drill into themselves, removing the
manual reporting step entirely.

## Outcome

Regional leads now check a live dashboard instead of waiting on a weekly
report, and the analyst who used to build it by hand was freed up for
higher-value work.
